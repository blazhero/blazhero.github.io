About

I wanted to play around with the code of an image I had found online. The image I used was a illustration created by one of my favorite artists, Alphonse Mucha. The idea was to create glitch art and see how different programs and codes can morph an image. We went into many different programs and converted the images a couple of times. I opened the image in notepad++ and deleted, or added, the code and letters I found from the data of the image.
