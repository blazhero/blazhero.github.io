About

In my video sound project, I wanted to tell a story of how people struggling with schizophrenia may experience in their daily lives. I decided to make a video like this, because I know some people that struggle with schizophrenia and need to take medication sometimes. I understand that this is not a correct representation, but it is trying to show how medicine that the people take can affect them in a negative way as well.
